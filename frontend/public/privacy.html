Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Walking Off Sunshine is a walking route finder that helps you find shaded paths based on the position of the sun. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Walking Off Sunshine does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • Location: Your device location is used only to set your starting point when you enable GPS. It is sent to our server solely to compute your walking route and is never stored, logged, or linked to any identifier.
  • No accounts: The app requires no registration or login.
  • No tracking: The app contains no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.
  • No retention: Route requests are discarded after the response is returned. Nothing is persisted on our servers beyond a short-lived route cache (maximum 20 minutes) keyed to anonymous origin/destination pairs.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the Google Maps API to display maps and autocomplete addresses. Google's handling of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Building and tree data is sourced from OpenStreetMap via the Overpass API. No personal data is sent to OpenStreetMap.

Children's Privacy

Walking Off Sunshine is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
